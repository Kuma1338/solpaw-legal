Privacy Policy

SolPaw Merge
Last Updated: February 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Kuma ("Developer", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how SolPaw Merge ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Wallet Address: When you connect your Solana wallet to make in-app purchases, your public wallet address is used to process transactions. We do not store your private keys.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality;
  • Usage Data: Game progress, session duration, and feature usage for improving the App experience;
  • Crash Reports: Technical logs to diagnose and fix issues.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal names, email addresses, or phone numbers;
  • Location data;
  • Private keys or wallet seed phrases;
  • Contacts or photos from your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process in-app purchases via the Solana blockchain;
  • Improve game performance and user experience;
  • Diagnose technical issues and fix bugs;
  • Ensure the security and integrity of the App.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only in the following cases:

  • Blockchain Transactions: Transaction data is publicly visible on the Solana blockchain by nature of the technology;
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights;
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized data may be used for analytics purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain usage data only as long as necessary to provide and improve the App. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

SolPaw Merge is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you;
  • Request correction or deletion of your data;
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data;
  • Data portability.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App or on this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: kuma@solpawmerge.dev
